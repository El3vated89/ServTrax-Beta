### Routes Module
- `routes_basic`: Core route building and viewing.
- `routes_optimization`: Auto-ordering from base camp.
- `routes_city_grouping`: Grouping stops by city.
- `routes_advanced_sorting`: Sorting by distance, due date, etc.
- `routes_live_tracking_future`: Real-time driver tracking (Future).
