# ServTrax Build Phases

---

## Phase 1 - MVP

- Customers.
- Service plans.
- Job tracking.
- Verification.
- Photos.
- Notes.
- GPS and timestamp capture.
- Smart messaging foundation.
- Shareable proof pages.
- Search and filtering foundation.

---

## Phase 2 - Routes And Core Expansion

- Manual routes.
- Assign customers/jobs to routes.
- Route data foundation.
- Payment tracking.
- Quoting system.
- Email support.
- Customer timeline.
- Customer portal foundation.

---

## Phase 3 - Equipment

- Equipment tracking.
- Maintenance logs.
- Stronger payment visibility.
- Expanded route tools.

---

## Phase 4 - Portal, Branding, And Payments

- Customer portal polish.
- Permanent links.
- Custom branding for Starter and above.
- Stripe payment processing.
- Payment links.
- More polished portal/payment flow.

---

## Phase 5 - AI

- AI messaging improvements.
- Smart lookup.
- AI search.
- Smart notes.
- Smart tasks.
- Route optimization.
- Automation groundwork.

---

## Phase 6 - Scale And Future Expansion

- Biz plan features.
- Bulk messaging.
- Automation.
- API access.
- Advanced reporting.
- External integrations.
- Multi-user support.

---

## Strategy

- Build foundation first.
- Enable features through feature flags.
- Keep updates backward compatible.
- Never rebuild the system for tier expansion.
- Keep route data structure early even if full route UI arrives later.
