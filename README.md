# ServTrax

**Track. Verify. Get Paid.**

ServTrax is a mobile-first field-service operations platform for small service businesses such as lawn care, pressure washing, contractors, and recurring service operators.

ServTrax is not intended to be a bloated CRM. It is built for speed in the field, minimal typing, camera-first verification, clear customer communication, and faster payment follow-through.

---

## Core Workflow

Track -> Verify -> Communicate -> Get Paid

- Track jobs and recurring services.
- Verify work with photos, timestamps, GPS, and notes.
- Generate ready-to-send customer messages.
- Share proof pages or customer portal views where enabled.
- Support payment visibility and payment collection flows.

---

## Core Features

- Customers and service plans.
- Job tracking.
- Verification records with photos, GPS, timestamp, and notes.
- Smart messaging with templates and Pro AI support.
- Quotes for Starter Lite and above.
- Customer portal and permanent links for Starter and above.
- Manual routes for core plans and smart routes for Pro.
- Equipment tracking for Starter Lite and above.
- Search and filtering for all plans, with AI search in Pro.
- Storage-controlled photo proof system with compression and retention.

## Pricing And Plans

ServTrax uses launch pricing to accelerate adoption.

### Free - $0

- Job tracking and verification.
- Template messaging.
- Manual routes.
- Full search and filtering.

Limitations:

- No quotes.
- No customer portal.
- Temporary links only.
- Stripe plus 0.5% ServTrax fee.
- "via ServTrax" branding.

### Starter Lite - $4 Launch / $5 Standard

- Quotes.
- Quote approval workflow.
- Equipment tracking.
- Maintenance logs.

Still includes:

- Stripe plus 0.5% ServTrax fee.
- No customer portal.
- No branding control.
- Temporary links only.

### Starter - $8 Launch / $10 Standard

- Customer portal.
- Permanent job links.
- Payment visibility.
- Custom branding.
- Stripe with no ServTrax transaction fee.

### Pro - $12 Launch / $20 Standard

- AI messaging.
- Smart routes.
- AI search.

### Biz - Future

- Bulk messaging.
- Automation.
- API access.
- Advanced reporting.
